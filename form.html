Delivery X

With the recent changes in the world over last two years during pandemic, Coronavirus impacted retailers of all types.

Which resulted the retailers to focus more on online presence and continue their business from anywhere.

But converting your business from physical to online is hectic task & require a lot of management & effort.

This is where We have got you covered.

DeliveryX enables your presence online & broaden your customer experience by allowing them to order food & grocery online. We provide your business the free mobile applications for iOS & Android , which allows you to order food and groceries online anytime from anywhere.
